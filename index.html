    Frequently Asked Questions

    The app uses GPS location to verify you're at the office when signing in or out. This ensures attendance accuracy and prevents fraudulent entries from outside the workplace.

    For Chrome/Android: Tap the lock icon in the address bar → Site settings → Location → Allow.

    For Safari/iOS: Go to Settings → Safari → Location → Ask or Allow. Then refresh this page.

    For Firefox: Tap the shield icon → Permissions → Location → Allow.

    This usually means:

    • Location services are turned off on your device - enable them in your phone settings
    • You're in airplane mode - turn off airplane mode
    • GPS signal is weak - try moving near a window or going outside
    • Browser doesn't have permission - check browser settings and allow location access

    After fixing, refresh the page.

    The app uses geofencing to ensure you're within the office perimeter. If you're at the office but getting this message:

    • Make sure you're inside the building, not in the parking lot
    • Check that GPS accuracy is high (wait a few moments for GPS to stabilize)
    • Try moving to a different spot with better GPS signal
    • Contact your admin if the office location needs updating

    GPS can take 10-30 seconds to get an accurate fix, especially:

    • Indoors or in basements
    • On first load after turning on location
    • In areas with poor satellite visibility

    Wait patiently - the status will update automatically once location is verified.

    Buttons are disabled until:

    • You've selected your name from the dropdown
    • GPS location has been verified
    • You're within the office geofence

    Once both conditions are met, the buttons will activate automatically.

    Yes! The app works offline. If you have no internet connection:

    • Your sign in/out will be saved locally on your device
    • The entry will show as "Pending sync" in your recent attendance
    • Once you regain internet, it will automatically sync to the server

    Your attendance is never lost, even without connection.

    The app automatically syncs your offline entries when:

    • You regain internet connection
    • You open the app again after being offline
    • The app detects you're back online (every 10 seconds when app is open)

    You don't need to do anything - sync happens automatically in the background.

    The app uses exponential backoff retry (8s, 16s, 32s, up to 60s). If sync continues to fail:

    • Check your internet connection
    • Make sure the server is accessible
    • Try refreshing the page
    • Your data is still saved locally and will sync when possible

    Pending offline entries: These are stored in IndexedDB and localStorage. Clearing browser data will remove them, so sync before clearing if possible.

    Synced entries: These are safely stored on the server and won't be affected by clearing browser data.

    Recommendation: Make sure you're online and the app shows "Synced" before clearing any browser data.

    Device ownership links a specific phone/tablet to your staff account. This prevents:

    • Employees sharing devices to fake attendance
    • Unauthorized people signing in from any device

    When you first sign in on a device, it becomes registered to you automatically.

    To use a new device:

    1. Get a reset code from your admin
    2. On the new device, try to sign in with your name
    3. When prompted, enter the reset code
    4. The device will be reassigned to you

    The old device will no longer be linked to your account.

    Contact your admin for a reset code, then:

    1. Install the app on your new phone
    2. Open the app and select your name
    3. Enter the reset code when prompted
    4. Your new device is now registered to you

    Your old phone will no longer be able to sign in as you.

    No, each staff member can only be registered to one device at a time. This is a security measure to prevent attendance fraud.

    If you need to switch devices, you must get a reset code from your admin to deregister the old device and register the new one.
